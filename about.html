Forbella Africa’s research hub.

              
Welcome to Forbella, Forbella Business Journal is the leading source for fresh perspectives and peer-reviewed research in business, entrepreneurship, and management from the African continent. Forbella also offers a global stage to authors and publishers where they can share their work with an engaged audience who are eager to explore new groundbreaking ideas. Join us in shaping the future of business and entrepreneurship in Africa-publish, learn, and lead with Forbella Business Journal.

Mission Statement

                    
To foster academic and professional discussions on business and entrepreneurship by providing high-quality, peer-reviewed content that addresses contemporary African management issues.

                  

                

              


              
              

                

                  

                    
                    
Vision

                    
To become the leading platform for business knowledge dissemination in Africa, bridging the gap between theory and practice.
